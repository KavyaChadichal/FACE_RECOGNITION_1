Chogada Singer Darshan Raval celebrates Birthday with Jacky Bhagnani, Prince Narula, Yuvika Chowdhary and Salim Merchant

By

1718

Singer Darshan Raval whose given two of the biggest chart busters in recent times, ‘Kamariya’ from Mitron and ‘Chogada’ from Loveyatri, threw his birthday cum success bash at a suburban nightspot on October 18 and the who’s who of the industry attended the event.

The party went on till the wee hours of the morning and was attended by Saleem Merchant, Rashmi Desai, Prince Narula and Yuvika Chaudhary along with Jacky Bhagnani, Aditi Singh Sharma and Naushad Khan, the managing director of Indie Music label among others.
